Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in starts at 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM.

Do you have WiFi and kitchen access

Yes, we provide free high-speed Wi-Fi and a fully equipped kitchen for self-cooking.

Can we book the whole villa for a group?

Absolutely! The Three-Bedroom Villa is perfect for group stays up to 10 guests.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers upon request. Additional charges may apply.

Are pets allowed at the villa?

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in our villa premises.

How can I modify or cancel my booking?

Please reach out to us via phone or email at least 48 hours in advance to modify or cancel bookings.
